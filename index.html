Delhi Project

Delhi Sewage Infrastructure Accountability Project

An RTI-based analysis of sewage treatment capacity, utilisation, expenditure, and governance gaps across Delhi Jal Board's 32 Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs).

What is the project?

Public infrastructure, read through RTI records.

The project examines treatment capacity, plant utilisation, expenditure disclosure, and governance gaps across Delhi Jal Board sewage infrastructure.

Why was it undertaken?

To make infrastructure performance legible.

The analysis turns plant-level RTI disclosures into a public-facing record of capacity use, underuse, overload, and disclosure quality.

What does it answer?

Where Delhi's treatment system is working, idle, strained, or opaque.

The report identifies utilisation patterns, financial disclosure gaps, operational anomalies, and governance risks across the STP network.

What I am building next.

These are the questions currently shaping the next phase of work: state elections, platform politics, political timing, voter response, and municipal governance data.

Open to research collaborations, data partnerships, field support, writing commissions, fellowships, and institutional homes for these projects.

Project 01

Social Media and News Discourse at State Election Level

A candidate- and constituency-level study of social media activity during state and assembly elections, mapped against news narratives to understand how political stories travel, mutate, and diverge across constituencies.

In development State elections Platform analysis

Project 02

The Electoral Clock: BJP's Political Timing Strategy, 2014-2029

A structured framework mapping major political decisions against election timelines. The working hypothesis is that high-appeal, low-risk decisions cluster close to elections while controversial moves are made earlier to allow recovery.

Framework design Political timing Predictive lens

Project 03

Electoral Timing and Voter Response

A second layer to the Electoral Clock project, connecting voter behaviour data, survey evidence, and socioeconomic indicators to test whether timing strategies shape outcomes across different regions and contexts.

Early stage Survey evidence Voter behaviour

Project 04

MCD Ward Governance Scorecard

A data-driven audit of Delhi municipal wards using public datasets, RTIs, and a pilot across 10-15 wards. The work begins with a data gap analysis that shapes the RTI strategy and scorecard design.

In development Delhi governance RTI strategy
